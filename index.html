Who we are

Hello, friend.

We warmly invite you to join our growing community of culture, health, and resilience. Promoting healing and connection to self and others, we are offering you billions of new friends with our ferments.

Our initial offering is a traditional local cabbage sauerkraut — crafted with care and offered at $12 donation/trade in the spirit of a circular economy, for a 16oz returnable jar.

We will be expanding our future batches to include seasonal crafted ferments, Chinese pickles, kimchis, vinegars, misos, and other bubbly creations. We would love for you to be part of our club and support our love for fermenting and creativity in our community.

❤️ The Brine Surgeons,
Catlin and Chris
