Who we are

Hello, friend.

We warmly invite you to join our club and growing community rooted in culture, health, and resilience. Promoting healing and connection to self and others, we are offering you billions of new friends through our ferments and craft goods.

Our club is just starting off with small seasonal batches, made when time and ingredients align. Please grow with us and have patience while we create and connect this community.

We will be expanding our future batches to include seasonal crafted ferments, Chinese pickles and fermented vegetables, kimchis, vinegars, misos, and many other bubbly creations. Future offerings may also include artisan goods like bone broths, tallow, honey, and other craft recipes.

We love what we do and are so excited to share it with our community.
